HPL Benches Suppliers In Dubai

HPL Benches are a system of customized and configured benches. They're meant to make the most, out of the space available for changing rooms and public areas. They're composed of mild steel sections. These are also powder coat finished, as their sturdy frame. 13mm thick High Pressure Laminated (HPL) are used as their planks. or slat formats (available in various colors)

Bench solution comprises a range of sturdy frame options which can be cleverly configured in different ways to maximize the space available. Having a variety of benches for changing rooms and public areas to complement the range of fixtures and fittings and manufactured with HPL waterproof in plank or slat formats. Each with smooth edges and available in a wide range of sporting colors

Wall-mounted benches, benches with shoe rack, hangers for clothes and bags, single and double units. Benches in standard sizes and with the option of manufacturing in any size on request by the customer. Changing room benches made with 13mm thickness compact grade laminate.

Each standard frame is fabricated in strong mild steel section and painted in a light grey powder coated finish making them suitable for use in wet or dry changing rooms. 40mm x 40mm powder coated tubular steel-based structure, finished off with black feet and legs which are adjustable in height.
